University of Chicago Repository

The University of Chicago Repository preserves and shares the scholarly, creative, and administrative assets of the University to support researchers, instructors, and staff.

Share

  • Share your research and meet funding agency requirements
  • Open up your lectures and symposia to new audiences
  • Create collections of teaching materials

Whether you want to share it with the campus, or share it with the world, the repository can handle various types of materials including articles, data, teaching & learning resources, dissertations, working papers, technical reports, lecture series and more.

Contribute now

Discover

  • Increase the visibility of your research through better exposure to search engines
  • Improve access to your non-published research (data, white papers, audio/video, conference presentations)
  • Get a permanent identifier for your research products (no more broken links!)

All content in the repository is structured and described in a way that ensures indexing in search engines and facilitates inclusion in other scholarly resources, increasing the discoverability of your materials. Identifiers (DOIs) will be minted for deposited content, giving your work a permanent home.

Preserve

  • Ensure your work is archived for future researchers
  • Satisfy preservation mandates from various funders
  • Manage long term access to your work

With both short and long term preservation options, the UC Repository uses standards that conform to funding agency requirements. The repository ensures the historical record of university life and scholarship will be preserved for future study.
